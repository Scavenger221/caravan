BLOXORZ

Get the block into the square hole.

  • Swipe (or use the arrows) to roll the block. Don't fall off the edge.
  • Orange tiles crack if the block stands upright on them.
  • Round switches trigger on any touch. X switches need the block standing on them. They open and close bridges.
  • Teleporters split the block into two halves — use ⇆ to swap between them. Rejoin them side by side.
  • The block must be standing upright to drop into the hole.
  • Misstep? ↩ takes back your last move — even a fatal one.
